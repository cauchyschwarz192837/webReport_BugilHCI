OBJECTIVES

1. To determine the ultraviolet B (UVB) absorbances and the sun protection factors (SPF) of anthocyanin and quercetin solutions

2. To investigate the radical scavenging activity (RSA) or antioxidant activity of anthocyanin and quercetin in the DPPH assay

3. To determine if anthocyanin and quercetin are able to increase the survival of E. coli exposed to UV radiation

4. To investigate if anthocyanin and quercetin are able to reduce tensile strength loss in hair exposed to UV radiation
