HYPOTHESIS

Anthocyanin and quercetin have significantly high UVB-absorbances, SPFs and RSAs, indicating great photoprotective capability and antioxidant activity.

Anthocyanin and quercetin can result in higher survival of E. coli exposed to UV radiation. Anthocyanin and quercetin are able to reduce tensile strength loss in hair exposed to UV radiation
