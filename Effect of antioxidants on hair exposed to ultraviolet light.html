Effect of antioxidants on hair exposed to ultraviolet light

Procedures

Five strands of hair were placed in each Petri dish and immersed in 0.1 mg/ml 90% quercetin, 10% anthocyanin solution, respectively. The control set-up consisted of sterile water. The Petri dishes were placed on top of the UV transilluminator and exposed to UV radiation for 1 min. Using a universal testing machine, the tensile strength of the hair was tested and compared with that of untreated hair.
